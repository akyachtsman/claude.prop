Methodology
WACC — the hurdle rate

Equity% × opportunity cost of equity + Debt% × cost of debt × (1 − tax rate). Here: 30% × 15% + 70% × 6.5% × 0.72 = 7.78%.

IRR — internal rate of return

The discount rate at which the NPV of the five-year series equals zero — the deal's expected annualized return. Given a constant CAP rate, IRR holds constant for any combination of NOI and purchase price.

NPV — net present value

NPV = Σ CFt / (1 + r)t − initial investment, discounted at WACC. A positive NPV means the deal's return exceeds the cost of capital — IRR will be greater than WACC, and the project clears the bar.
